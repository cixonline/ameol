(Route : CIX Forums Menu,Edit My Resume Command)

The Edit My Resume dialog box allows you to edit your resume.



This resume is held on CIX Forums and is accessible by all other users of CIX Forums. You can put any information that you like into this resume such as your full name, telephone number, career details, any alternate email addresses or details of your web site.

The main area of this dialog shows the contents of your resume as it is currently held on CIX Forums. You can directly edit this text and when you have finished, click the Save button.

Your resume will be uploaded to CIX Forums the next time you connect.
