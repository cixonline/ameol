A forum is a subject area within CIX Forums where messages are posted which can be read by all members of the forum. CIX Forums exist in three forms - open (anybody can join), closed (forum will be listed but you can only be joined by a moderator) and closed confidential (not listed).

There are lots of forums on CIX Forums covering all manner of subjects and each of these is overseen by a moderator. A CIX forum can also hold files that can be downloaded by members of the forum.

For a full list of CIX forums select the Show All Forums from the CIX Forums Menu.
