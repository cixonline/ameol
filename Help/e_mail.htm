E-mail
Previous  Top  Next


E-mail refers to messages that are sent to one or more separate e-mail addresses and cannot be read by anyone other than the recipient(s) of the message. For instance, if you send an e-mail message to joebloggs@cix.co.uk only Joe will be able to read your message. If you post a message to a CIX forum or a Usenet Newsgroup, then anybody who is a member of or subscribes to that forum or newsgroup will be able to read your message.
