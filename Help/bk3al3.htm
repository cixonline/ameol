(Route : CIX Forums Menu, Join Forum)

The Join Forum dialog box allows you to join a CIX forum.



This is a Hot Spot image. Move your mouse pointer over the image
and when the mouse pointer changes to the shape of a hand click
the left mouse button once for extra help.


Enter the name of the forum that you wish to join in the Forum text box and a particular topic (if you don't want to join them all) in the Topic text box.

You also have the option of choosing how many messages you would like to download when you join by entering a number in the Recent Messages on Join text box. The default is 100.

Click the OK button to add an item to the Out Basket. You will be joined to the forum the next time that Ameol connects to CIX Forums.
