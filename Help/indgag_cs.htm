Welcome to Ameol!


Ameol is an all-in-one off-line reader for your Internet news, mail and CIX Forums messages. As it is an off-line reader you can read and compose messages without being connected to your service provider or on-line service, so saving time and money. Ameol can be used with an Internet account to read usenet news and mail and also to read CIX Forums messages.

If at any time when using Ameol you are not sure what to do please click on the Help buttons. This is a context-sensitive help file which means that the help file will open on the correct page for the screen where you pressed Help.

Many of the graphic files within this Help file are Hot Spot images. This means you can move your mouse pointer over them and your pointer will change to a picture of a hand. When it does this click the left mouse button once for extra help on the item selected.
