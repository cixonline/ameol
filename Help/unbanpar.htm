Unban Participant Dialog Box
Previous  Top  Next 


(Route : CIX Forums Menu, Participants, Unban Button)



The Unban Participant dialog box allows you to remove the ban on participant from a conference that you moderate. Use the associated Ban Participant button to ban a participant.

Enter the CIX Forum nickname of the participant to be un-banned in the Enter participant name text box.

Click OK to un-ban the participant the next time Ameol connects to CIX Forums.
